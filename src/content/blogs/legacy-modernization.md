# Modernizing Legacy Systems with Next.js

Legacy systems are more than just old code; they are the foundation of many successful businesses. But as user expectations grow, these systems can become a bottleneck.

## Steps to Modernization
1. **Assessment**: Identify the most critical bottlenecks.
2. **Hybrid Migration**: Use Next.js middleware to route traffic between the old and new systems.
3. **API First**: Extract business logic into specialized microservices.

> "The best time to refactor was yesterday. The second best time is today."

### Why Next.js?
Next.js provides the perfect balance of static generation for speed and server-side rendering for dynamic data, making it an ideal target for large-scale migrations.
